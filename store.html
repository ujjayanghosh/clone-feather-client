Store Update!

Brendan
Aug 22, 2024 -1 min read

Today we're introducing an update to our store! Rather than purchasing items through our store on website, you will now purchase points in-game. Afterwards, you can spend the points on cosmetics!

Packages for points will scale up, so the more points you purchase, the more "bonus" points you will receive, up to 28%! Additionally, we've introduced multi-currency options that make cosmetics more affordable for Europeans.

To celebrate, we will be having a **10% OFF SALE** for the whole store until August 31st, thank you everyone for the support!
